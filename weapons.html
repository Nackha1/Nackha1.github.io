Monster Hunter: World Weapons About

The Hunter's Arsenal

There are fourteen different weapons at the hunter's disposal, each with its own unique characteristics and attacks. Many hunters acquire proficiency in multiple types, while others prefer to attain mastery of one.

A total of 14 Weapons are in the game: 11 Blademaster Weapons and 3 Gunner Weapons.
  1. Great Sword: Large, powerful but slow heavy-damagers
  2. Sword & Shield: Balanced, mobile & beginner friendly
  3. Dual Blades: Light but extremely fast flurry of attacks.
  4. Long Sword: Nimble and with great melee range
  5. Hammer: Slow but heavy & stunning attacks
  6. Hunting Horn: Easy and long range support weapon
  7. Lance: Strong Defense and Offense
  8. Gunlance: High offense, limited range
  9. Switch Axe: Versatile, fast & elemental damage.
  10. Charge Blade: Versatile, powerful and fast
  11. Insect Glaive: Mobile, aerial attacks and buffs
  12. Bow: Fast, ranged debuff attacks.
  13. Light Bowgun: High mobility, rapid fire ranged weapon
  14. Heavy Bowgun: Slow but powerful ranged attacks.
